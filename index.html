FoodSup

View FoodSup GitHub Repo

My work at the gabba involves making sure all the food goes out to the customers at the correct times, while dealing with time changes, dietaries, late arrivals and more. Usually, this is all done by highlighting a list of food on paper, writing when the food went out and managing time changes manually. This program was made to make it easier and more manageable to do this work. As a result I records of when and where the food went are far more accurate, and taking the food out is quicker as I was more involved in preparation due to the help of the program.
